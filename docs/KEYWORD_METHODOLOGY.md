### Step 3: Search Intent Classification

I categorize every keyword by buyer intent:

#### 🔴 HIGH INTENT (Ready to Buy)

**Characteristics**:

- Specific product type
- Includes strength/dosage
- Brand names

**Examples**:

- `dihydroberberine capsules`
- `berberine 500mg`
- `glucovantage supplement`

**Strategy**:

- Match Type: Exact or Phrase
- Bid: HIGHEST (80-100% of max bid)
- Expected CVR: 10-15%
- Target ACOS: 25-30%

---

#### 🟡 MEDIUM INTENT (Researching Solutions)

**Characteristics**:

- Benefit-focused
- Problem-solving
- Comparison terms

**Examples**:

- `berberine for blood sugar`
- `blood sugar support supplement`
- `natural glucose control`

**Strategy**:

- Match Type: Phrase
- Bid: MEDIUM (60-80% of max bid)
- Expected CVR: 6-10%
- Target ACOS: 35-45%

---

#### 🟢 LOW INTENT (Learning/Researching)

**Characteristics**:

- Question-based
- Educational
- General information

**Examples**:

- `berberine benefits`
- `what is dihydroberberine`
- `berberine side effects`

**Strategy**:

- Match Type: Exact Negative (Block these!)
- OR: Very low bid if building brand awareness
- Expected CVR: 1-3%
- Target ACOS: >60% (not profitable)

### Step 4: Keyword Volume vs Competition Analysis

For each keyword, I analyze:

**Search Volume** (Monthly searches):

- High: 10,000+ (competitive, expensive)
- Medium: 1,000-10,000 (sweet spot)
- Low: <1,000 (cheap but low traffic)

**Competition Level**:

- High: 500+ advertisers (CPC $1.50+)
- Medium: 100-500 advertisers (CPC $0.80-$1.50)
- Low: <100 advertisers (CPC $0.30-$0.80)

**The Matrix**:

```
High Volume + High Competition = Branded/Generic terms
- Example: "berberine supplement"
- Strategy: Bid cautiously, monitor ACOS closely

High Volume + Low Competition = GOLDEN OPPORTUNITY
- Example: "dihydroberberine capsules"
- Strategy: Bid aggressively, scale budget

Low Volume + Low Competition = Long-tail terms
- Example: "berberine 500mg vegan capsules"
- Strategy: Low bids, harvest cheap conversions

Low Volume + High Competition = AVOID
- Poor ROI
```

---

### Step 5: Bid Calculation Formula

I calculate bids based on YOUR specific metrics:

**Formula**:

```
Max Bid = (Product Price × Target ACOS %) × Expected CVR

Example:
- Product Price: $30
- Target ACOS: 30%
- Expected CVR: 10%

Max Bid = ($30 × 0.30) × 0.10 = $0.90
```

**Adjustments by Match Type**:

- Exact Match: $0.90 × 1.0 = **$0.90**
- Phrase Match: $0.90 × 0.8 = **$0.72**
- Broad Match: $0.90 × 0.6 = **$0.54**

**Adjustments by Placement**:

- Top of Search: +50% ($1.35)
- Product Pages: +0% ($0.90)
- Rest of Search: -20% ($0.72)
