## 9. KEYWORD RESEARCH METHODOLOGY I'LL USE

Once you provide the above information, I will:

### Step 1: Core Product Keywords

- Extract keywords from your title
- Identify ingredient-based keywords
- Map benefit-based keywords

### Step 2: Search Intent Analysis

- **High Intent** (Exact Match): Buyers ready to purchase
  - Example: "[ingredient] [product type]"
  - Bid strategy: Higher bids, exact match
  
- **Medium Intent** (Phrase Match): Researching specific benefits
  - Example: "[ingredient] for [benefit]"
  - Bid strategy: Medium bids, phrase match
  
- **Low Intent** (Research): Learning about category
  - Example: "[ingredient] benefits"
  - Bid strategy: Lower bids or exclude

### Step 3: Competitor Keyword Mining

- Analyze competing ASINs
- Identify gaps in competitor targeting
- Find "conquest" opportunities

### Step 4: Negative Keyword Identification

- Price qualifiers (cheap, discount)
- Wrong product type (powder if you sell capsules)
- Informational queries (side effects, reddit reviews)

### Step 5: Bid Calculation Formula

```
Base Bid = (Target ACoS × Average Order Value) / Expected CVR
- Exact Match: Base Bid × 1.0
- Phrase Match: Base Bid × 0.8
- Broad Match: Base Bid × 0.6
```

### Step 6: Match Type Distribution

- **Exact**: 40% of keywords (high-intent, proven terms)
- **Phrase**: 50% of keywords (discovery + medium-intent)
- **Broad**: 10% of keywords (research phase only)
